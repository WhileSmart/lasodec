# USER MANUAL
This document describes the major features of the app and how to use them.

## SECTION A: Dashboard Users
Dashboard users are the administrators of the app who can manage users, roles, permissions, CSOs and other aspects of the app.

### Dashboard landing and statistics
The dashboard landing page shows an overview of the app's performance and statistics, such as the number of users, CSOs, and experts. You can access the dashboard landing page by logging in with your dashboard credentials, or by clicking on the title found at the top left corner of the dashboard page.
![Dashboard landing page](docs/../images/dashboard-main.png)

### Managing Users

You can view, edit, delete and create users from the Users page. To access the Users page, click on the Users link in the navigation bar.

![Users page](docs/../images/dashboard-users.png)

On the Users page, you can see a table with all the users in the app. You can use the table filters to filter the users by name, or email. You can also use the pagination buttons to navigate through the pages of users.

To edit a user, click on menu button then the Edit button next to their name. This will open a page  where you can change their name, email, password, and role. To save your changes, click on the Update button.

![Edit user page](docs/../images/dashboard-users-edit.png)

To delete a user, click on the Delete button in the menu next to their name. This will open a confirmation dialog where you have to agree in order to proceed.

![Delete user dialog](docs/../images/dashboard-users-delete.png)

To create a new user, click on the Add button at the top right of the Users page. This will open a page where you have to enter their name, email, password and role. To create the user, click on the Save button.

![Create user page](docs/../images/dashboard-users-add.png)

## Section B: Non-dashboard Users

Non-dashboard users are the regular users of the website who can create accounts, register CSOs or as experts, read blog posts and join events.

### Account creation, login and password resets

Users can create an account in the website. Users need to create an account with first name, last name, email, country and password. You can create an account by clicking on the Login link at the top of the  navigation bar and in the login page click on the Sign up link.

![Sign up page](docs/../images/website-signup.png)

After creating your account, you can log in with your email and password by clicking on the Log In link in the navigation bar.

![Log in page](docs/../images/website-login.png)

If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking on the Forgot Password link on the Log In page. This will open a page where you have to enter your email and click on the Send Password Reset Link button. You will receive an email with a link to reset your password.

![Forgot password page](docs/../images/website-forgot-password.png)

### CSO Registration

If you are a representative of a civil society organization (CSO), you can register your CSO in the website. To register your CSO, you need to log in with your account, navigate to the CSO directory page and click on the Register CSO button.

![Register CSO link](docs/../images/website-register-cso.png)

This will open a page where you have to fill in a form with your CSO's details, such as name, registration, location, description and logo. To submit your registration, click on the Confirm CSO registration button.

![Register CSO form](docs/../images/website-register-cso-form.png)

After submitting your registration, you will see a confirmation message that your CSO has been registered and is pending approval.

### Expert Profile Registration

If you are an expert in a certain field or topic, you can register your profile in the website. To register your profile, you need to log in with your account, navigate to the Experts directory page and click on the Register Expert Profile button.

![Register Expert link](docs/../images/website-expert-button.png)

This will open a page where you have to fill in a form with your profile details, such as position, bio, skills, experience and location. To submit your registration, click on the Confirm your Registration button.

![Register Expert form](docs/../images/website-register-expert.png)
![Register Expert form](docs/../images/website-register-expert-2.png)

After submitting your registration, you will see a confirmation message that your profile has been registered and is pending approval.

### Events

The events feature allows you to discover and join events related to civil society and social change. You can access the events by clicking on the Events link in the navigation bar.

On the events page, you can see a list of the upcoming events. You can also use the pagination buttons to navigate through the pages of events.

To view an event, click on its title or image. This will open the event page where you can see the type, date, time and description of the event. You can also register for the event by filling out the form in the event's page and clicking on the Confirm Event Participation button.
